Great news: as of today, every piece of content on this site is available in **both English and Chinese**!

<!--more-->

## What changed

There's now a language toggle in the **top-right corner** of every page (`Zh` / `En`). Click it to switch freely between Chinese and English — and it takes you to the translation of **the exact page you're on**, rather than dumping you back at the home page.

From now on, any native English speaker can read this site comfortably: the blog posts, the physics problem sets, and the IELTS speaking materials all have English versions.

## A few notes

- The site defaults to Chinese; the English version lives under the `/en/` path.
- Every new piece of content will be published in both languages, with identical content.
- English pages never mix in Chinese. If some material is inherently in English (like the English-study section), it simply shows in English in both modes.

Thanks for reading, as always. If you have English-speaking friends, feel free to share the link with them now 🎉

> Welcome aboard, English readers — hit the `En` toggle in the top-right corner and enjoy!
